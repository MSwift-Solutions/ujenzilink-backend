# UjenziLink Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 13 March 2026

Karibu UjenziLink! This document explains how we collect and use your data when you use our platform for your *mjengo* projects and networking.

## 1. Information We Collect
We only collect info that you directly provide to us or generate while using the app. This includes:
* **Your Profile Info:** Your name, email, bio, and login details.
* **Your Projects:** Details about your construction projects, including titles, stages, costs, and project documents.
* **Your Activity:** Posts you create, images you upload, bookmarks, and your likes or comments on projects and posts.
* **Your Interactions:** We keep track of the projects you follow and the professional connections (collaborators/members) you make.
* **Your Chats:** Direct messages and group conversations you have with other users on the platform.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
We use the info above strictly to:
* Make the app work for you (managing your projects, tracking expenses, and showing your progress).
* Enable you to socialize, post updates, and chat with other members.
* Send you important email notifications regarding your account or projects.

## 3. Public vs. Private Projects
You have control over your project's visibility. 
* If you set your project or posts to **Public**, other users on the platform can view your updates, images, and progress.
* If you set them to **Private**, only the people you specifically invite as project members can see the details.

## 4. Keeping Your Data Safe
We do our best to protect your information through secure servers and encrypted passwords. We don't sell your personal details to anyone. We keep your data only as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account, you can do so, and we will permanently remove your data.

For any questions about your privacy, kindly email us at **privacy@ujenzilink.com**.
